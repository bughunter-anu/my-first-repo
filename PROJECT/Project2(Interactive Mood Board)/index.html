"Happiness is a warm sunshine.

The phrase "Happiness is a warm sunshine" highlights the idea that happiness can be found in simple, everyday things, like the feeling of warm sunlight on your skin. It emphasizes the positive impact that even small things, like a sunny day, can have on one's mood and sense of well-being. The phrase is often used to evoke feelings of joy, comfort, and contentment.

Peace begins with a deep breath.

"Peace begins with a deep breath" is a popular saying that emphasizes the connection between breathing and inner peace. It suggests that taking a moment to focus on your breath can help you find calm and clarity amidst the chaos of life. This idea is echoed in various wellness practices and mindfulness techniques.
